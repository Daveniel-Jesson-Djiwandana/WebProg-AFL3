HOBBY

Over the years, I have had the same hobby which is reading, specifically manhwas (Korean comics). They have been a big part of my life where almost every second of my free time is reading these manhwas. I have read more than 400 titles and have finished more than a hundred of those titles. The concept of building a world, not only it’s landscape, but every creature and resource in that world, is why I enjoy manhwas very much.
